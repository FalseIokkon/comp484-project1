Professional Leagues

There are many professional leagues around the world, similar to how the NBA, MLB, and NFL work within the United States. The largest international league is governed by the ITTF and is called the World Table Tennis Organization. The WTT hosts the most prestigious events throughout the year, although they are not the only ones to host professional players from around the world. The Chinese Super League, Major League Table Tennis, and the Bundesliga are all examples of leagues within a single country.

Chinese Super League

at 50/100

The Table Tennis Super League (CTTSL) is the premier professional table tennis league in China, widely regarded as the strongest in the world. It attracts top Chinese national players as well as international stars, and its high level of competition makes it a major stage for showcasing elite talent and developing the next generation of champions.

World Table Tennis Association

at 50/100

The Table Tennis (WTT) Association is the commercial and events arm of the ITTF, created to modernize and promote the sport globally. It organizes major competitions like the Grand Smashes and Champions series, showcasing top players and bringing table tennis to a wider audience.
